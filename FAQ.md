**How should I get to Novi Sad?**
Novi Sad is just over an hour's drive away from the Belgrade airport. We recommend booking a Taxi or car service.

**Is there a dress code?**
Formal dress is kindly requested for the wedding - long dresses or dressy separates for women and dark suits with ties for men. There is no dress code for the welcome dinner
